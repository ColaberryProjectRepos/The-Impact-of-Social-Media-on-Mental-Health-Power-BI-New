Selection: Multi-select with C... Switch to "on". The other two options should be turned to "Off"
Slicer header: Switch to "Off"
Values:

Color: #0D6ABF

Properties: Size: Height: 70 and Width: 170
Position: Horizontal: 1110 and Vertical: 0
Title: Switch "On"
Text: "Social Media Time"
Font: Times New Roman, size 12
Text color: #FFFFFF
Background color: #0D6ABF
Horizontal alignment: center
Effects:
Background: "On"
Color: #FFFFFF
Transparency: 15%
