Creating Background, Title, and Logos 12

1. First, we will add the background. Click on "Canvas Background" from the Visualizations panel. For the image, choose the "effects-of-social-media-on-your-mental-health.webp" from the zip file provided earlier. Make the following changes:
Image fit: Fill
 Transparency: 25%
2. Next, add the logos. Click on "Insert" from the top panel next to "Home." Click on "Image" and then choose "Colaberry Logo." Do the following changes:
Properties: Height: 49 and Width: 200
Position: Horizontal: 0 and Vertical 0
Background: "On"
Do the same steps again but insert "Effects of Social Media on Mental Health." Do the following changes to the image:
Properties: Height: 113 and Width: 200
Position: Horizontal: 0 and Vertical: 49
3. Now we will add the title. Click on the "Insert" button from the top banner and select "Text Box" towards the right end of the insert options banner. For the title, type "The Effects of Social Media on Mental Health" Choose Times New Roman font, size 24, align it to the center and color = #0D6ABF. After doing that, make the following changes from the "Format" panel on the right side of the screen:
Properties:
Size: Height: 70 and Width: 909
Position Horizontal: 200 and Vertical: 0
Effects:
Background: "On", Color: #FFFFFF, Transparency: 15%
Visual Border: "On", Color: #CCCCCC
