ROI Scenario Based on Confirmed Stats from my Dataset:
Assumption: An 8% reduction in distractibility leads to a 4% increase in productivity.
Industry Average: Each hour of increased productivity is worth $45.
Calculation: For a person working 8 hours per day, a 4% increase in productivity equates to 0.32 extra productive hours, or $14.4 per day.
Annual ROI: $14.4/day * 250 working days/year = $3,600 increase in productivity per individual per year.
Connection to Case: Considering the confirmed dataset insights, a targeted intervention aimed at reducing distractibility by 8% in high social media users aged 18-29 can potentially result in a 4% increase in productivity. Based on industry averages, this intervention offers a considerable ROI, highlighting the potential benefits of addressing distractibility in this demographic
