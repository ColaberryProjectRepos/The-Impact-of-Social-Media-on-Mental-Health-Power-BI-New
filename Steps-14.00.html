Creating KPI Cards 14


In this step we will create the 5 KPI percentage cards starting with "Sleep Issues Frequency" KPI card.
1. "Sleep Issues Frequency" KPI: Copy and paste the "Last Refresh Date" card that you just created. First, you will need to change the data. Unselect "Last Refresh Date" and add "Sleep Issues Frequency Index" from the Data panel on the far right of the report. In the Visualizations panel under "Visual", do the following changes:
Callout values: Font: Times New Roman, size 30, color: #73361C, alignment: center
Switch from "Visual" to "General" in the Visualizations panel. Do the following changes:
Properties:
Size: Height: 79 and Width: 200
Position: Horizontal: 0 and Vertical: 572
Padding: Top: "0 px", sides and bottom: "5 px"
Title:
Text: "Sleep Issues Frequency", font: Times New Roman, size 12, Text color: #FFFFFF, Background color: #0D6ABF, Horizontal alignment: center
Effects:
Background: Switch to: "On"
color: #FFFFFF, Transparency: 15%
Visual border: "On", color: #CCCCCC
Now we will add an indicator. Click on the "Insert" button again and select "Shapes" next to the "Image" element that you selected earlier. Choose the basic rectangle from the available shapes. We want to make sure it sits on top of the KPI card so that it is always visible. Click on "Format" from the top banner and then click "Bring Forward" and then "Bring to Front" from the two options that appear. Next, click on the "Format" panel on the right of the report and make the following changes:
Rotation:
All: 45 degrees
Style:
Fill: choose Custom:
Format style: rules
What field should we base this on?: Sleep Issues Frequency Index
If value: ">=", "Min" and "<", "0.55", then color "#00F731", click "+New rule",
If value: ">=", "0.55" and "<", "0.65", then color "#FFFF00", click "+New rule",
If value: ">=", 0.65" and "<", "Max", then color "#FE0000"
Click "Ok" and then switch the border from "on" to "Off" while still in the style tab.
The indicator will now show if the Sleep issues frequency is in a good, bad, or neutral range. Now Switch from "Shape" to "General" in Format panel on the right side of the report. Make the following changes:
Properties:
Size: Height: 25 and Width: 25
Position: Horizontal: 156 and Vertical: 612.
The First KPI card is now completed. In order to make the remaining cards and indicators, that card can be copied and pasted with just a few minor changes.
