Creating Date Refresh 13

1. First, we will create the refresh card. Select the "Card" visualization to add it to the report, then add the "Last Refresh Date" to the "Data" box. You should see the refresh date on the card that you just added. Now it is time to format it. On the right of the report page, click "Format Visual" in the Visualizations pane. Click on "Callout Values" and change the following:
Font: Times New Roman, size 12
Horizontal Alignment: Center
Label: Switch from "on" to "off"
2. Next, scroll down to "Cards" and click on that.
Click padding and change the "size" to custom and set it so that the bottom and two sides are "4 px" and the top is "0 px"
Click on Background and change the "Transparency" to 50%.
Switch Border to "Off"
3. Switch from "Visual" to "General" towards the top of the Visualizations panel, just under the search bar. Click on "Properties" and change to the following:
Size: Height = 68, Width = 200.
Position: Horizontal = 0, Vertical = 652
Padding: Bottom and sides = "5 px", top = "0 px"
4. Switch "Title" to "on" and click on it. Do the following:
Text: Type "Last Refresh Date"
Font: Times New Roman, 12 pt
Text color: "#FFFFFF"
Background color: Blue "#0D6ABF"
Horizontal Alignment: Center
5. Click on "Effects" and do the following:
Background: Transparency = 15%
Visual Border: Switch to "On" and make the color "#CCCCCC"
