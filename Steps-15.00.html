Creating Number of People KPI 15

1. Copy and paste the "Depression Frequency" KPI card. Change the data field from "Average Depression Frequency" to "Number of People". Now, make the following changes in "Format Visual":
Callout Values:
Font: change color to: #000000
Switch from "Visual" to "General":
Properties:
Size: Height: 91 and Width: 200
Position: Horizontal: 0 and Vertical: 162.
Title:
Text: change it to "Number of People"
Once you are finished setting up the KPI card, send it backward by clicking on "Format from the banner near the top of your screen, then select "Send Backward"
2. Now, we will create two sub KPIs on either side of the count of the number of people. The left side will show the percentage of Males and the right side will show the percentage of females.
Insert a new card into the report from the "Visualizations" panel. Choose the "Total Males" measure for the data. Next, click on "Format Visual" and change the following:
Callout Values:
Values:
Font: Times New Roman, Size: 15, Color: #000000
Horizontal Alignment: Center
Label: Switch from "On" to "Off"
Cards:
Padding:
Size: Custom, Top "0 px", Sides "4 px", bottom "12 px"
Background: Switch from "on" to "off"
Border: Switch from "on" to "off"
Switch from "Visual" to "General"
Properties:
Size: Height: 60 and Width: 60
Position: Horizontal: 0 and Vertical: 189
Padding Top "0 px" and sides and bottom "5 px"
Title: Switch to "On"
Text: Males
Font: Times New Roman, size 10
Color: #000000
Horizontal alignment: center
Effects:
Background: Switch from "On" to "Off"
3. Female sub KPI. In order to make this sub KPI, copy and paste the "Male" sub KPI that you just created and change the data from "Total Males" to "Total Females", then make the following format changes:
Properties:
Position: Horizontal: 139 and Vertical: 189
Title:
Text: change it to "Females"
